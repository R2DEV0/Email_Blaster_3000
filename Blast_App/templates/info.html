Gmail App Password Instructions



1. Go to MyAccount.google.com. You may have to log in to your Gmail account if not already



2. Click on "Security" on the left side-bar



3. Click on "App Passwords" in the middle options section



4. You will see the "your app passwords page". Click on "Select app" and choose "Mail"

4b. Then click on "select device" (I use windows for this example). Then select "generate"


5. You will then receive a Gmail App Password. Copy and paste that into the Email Blaster 3000! (Note: Your password will not be saved in the Email Blaster for your security)
